## What Is This

Loan Capital Track is a private, subscription-based platform built for:

- Private lenders managing multiple borrowers
- Small credit unions needing digital infrastructure
- Family offices tracking private debt positions
- Mortgage brokers managing loan pipelines

This isn't a demo. It's production software that handles real capital.

---

## What You Get

### Portfolio Dashboard
See everything at a glance — total exposure, active loans, incoming payments, and portfolio health metrics. Charts update in real-time.

### Borrower Management
Each borrower gets a profile with credit score tracking, risk classification, and complete payment history. Know who's paying on time and who's not.

### Payment Tracking
Automated payment schedules with status tracking. Overdue payments are flagged immediately. Mark payments as paid with one click.

### Document Vault
Store loan agreements, IDs, promissory notes, and collateral documents. Everything tied to the right loan, accessible in seconds.

### Email Automation
Send overdue notices and payment reminders without lifting a finger. Professional templates, automatic delivery.

### Bilingual Support
Full English and Spanish interfaces. Switch instantly. Your borrowers can see their info in their language.

## FAQ

**Can I export my data?**
Yes. Full CSV export available for all loans, payments, and borrowers. Your data belongs to you.

**What happens if I cancel?**
You can export everything before cancellation. Data is retained for 30 days, then permanently deleted.

**Do you offer white-label options?**
For high-volume lenders (100+ loans), we offer custom branding. Contact us.

**Is there an API?**
Not yet. On the roadmap for Q2 2026.
